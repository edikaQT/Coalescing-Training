
Files to replicate the figures and tables in 

Quispe-Torreblanca E, Stewart N, Birnbaum M

Surprisingly Robust Violations of Stochastic Dominance Despite Coalescing Training: A Quasi-Adversarial Collaboration


 
**************

These files replicate all of the figues and tables in the paper.

Set the globals at the top of the file to your local directory locations. 

Experimental materials are available at https://github.com/neil-stewart/stoc_dom_2 

A video of the experimental task can be found at https://github.com/neil-stewart/stoc_dom_2/blob/main/screenshots/toggle.mp4.

**************

"200423 raw_data_cleaning.do" cleans the raw data.

"200423 analysis.do" reproduce all regressions, figues and tables presented in the paper.
